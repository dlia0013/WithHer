WithHer — Product Requirements Document
    "You're not alone here — navigating Australian health, together."


1. The problem
Young migrant women arriving in Australia have no guidance on how the health system works. Their parents can't help. Google is overwhelming. Most health platforms assume you already know the system — WithHer doesn't.

2. Target user
Young migrant women in Australia (18–30) — international students, recent migrants — who feel confused by Medicare, embarrassed to ask basic questions, and completely alone navigating the system.

3. Core features (MVP)
Feature -- Priority
"New to Australia?" onboarding guide -- High
Find Care — filter by bulk billing, language, telehealth -- High
Health Guides — plain language explainers -- High
Age-based health checklist -- High
Book appointment + email confirmation -- Medium
My Health Hub -- Medium

4. Design principles
Warm, not clinical
Plain language always — no jargon
Mobile-first
Inclusive — not pink/gendered, but human

5. Rework roadmap

✅ Phase 1 — Foundation (folder structure, Tailwind, brand)
🔄 Phase 2 — UI rework page by page
⬜ Phase 3 — New features (onboarding, health guides)
⬜ Phase 4 — Polish (mobile, Pinia, performance)
